Program Sections

This club includes training, youth development, fan engagement, and community outreach programs.

Junior Program

The junior program cultivates young talent, providing elite coaching, skill development, and a pathway to professional play, fostering a strong foundation for future success.

Teenager Program

The teenager program focuses on honing skills, promoting discipline, and fostering teamwork among aspiring athletes, preparing them for elite competition and potential professional careers in hockey.

Professional Program

The professional program emphasizes elite training, strategic gameplay, and performance optimization, cultivating a winning culture to compete at the highest level in professional hockey leagues.
